About Me

Hello! I’m Sohorab Mahmud Fahim, a passionate web developer and technology enthusiast currently studying at Bangladesh Noubahini College Dhaka. I’m in Grade 11, pursuing my academic and technological aspirations side by side.

I specialize in creating engaging and user-friendly web experiences. My journey into the world of technology started with curiosity, and over the years, I’ve developed expertise in web development, graphic designing, and robotics.

As a professional web developer, I have experience working with front-end and back-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP, and Python. Currently, I’m diving into native app development and exploring the exciting world of cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

In addition to coding, I have a creative side that enjoys designing graphics and 3D models. I’m also an active member of various tech communities, serving as the Vice President of ICT at the Science and Technology Club of my college. My role allows me to inspire and mentor others while contributing to innovative projects.

When I’m not coding or working on tech projects, you can find me singing my favorite songs or improving my English fluency. I believe that continuous learning and staying curious are keys to personal and professional growth.
